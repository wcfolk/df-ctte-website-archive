Sending emails

More complicated than you think!

Due to a minor fuck up, we can't send emails from @spanthatworld.com accounts. You can send emails from @spanthat.world though, and should already receive emails sent to role@spanthat.world

To set up sending them, got to Gmail online and click on Setting --> See all settings

Navigate to the Accounts tab and under Send Mail As click Add another email address

In the new window, type in your_role@spanthat.world in the Email address: box, then click Next Steps.

You should now be set up to send mail from that address

If you want to sign in on an external email client like Outlook or Thunderbird, then give me a call/message on 07523485079, on Slack or via email
